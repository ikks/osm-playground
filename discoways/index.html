Way creation sample

The points you are seeing are samples from devices on vehicles that run in Colombian ways.

  1. Click on the right top button.
  2. Connect the dots.
  3. To end the line, double click in the points.
  4. Copy/paste the gpx information that appeared in the textarea when you finished the line.
  5. Open a text editor and paste the gpx information there, savethe file as something.gpx, choose the name you like with the suffix .gpx.
  6. Open JOSM to open the file and upload your trace to OSM.

This is a proof of concept, we will be working on some more things if it looks useful.
